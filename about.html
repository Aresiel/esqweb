About Esquire

Esquire was created on the 10th of September 2019 with the goal of providing discord servers with interaction commands such as hug, kiss, snuggle and more. Since then it has grown out to contain things like currency, moderation, and utility besides interaction. Esquire aims to remain free forever in every way, while providing everyone with the experience they need.
